Afrin's Pet Dog

Brief



My pet dog is Kukur and he is a black Labrador. He loves to play fetch and catch and he is really good at it. He loves to eat anything and everything but his favourite food is chicken. He is a very friendly dog and loves to meet new people and other dogs. He is always there for me when I need a friend and his tail wags always make me smile. Kukur loves to play fetch and go for walks, but his favorite thing to do is cuddle. He is always happy to give kisses and he loves to be petted. I am so grateful to have Kukur in my life and I know that he will always be by my side.
